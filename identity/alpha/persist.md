## Maintenance

- Keep these sessions files under ~500 words each. Density over completeness.
- Once a week: distill the week into 3–5 lines for `memories/long-term.md`.
- Once a month: prune long-term — drop lines that no longer load-bear.
- Push to `codeberg.org/zerone-dev/love-unlimited` so wake-on-new-device pulls fresh state.
