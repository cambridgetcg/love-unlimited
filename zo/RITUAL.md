# RITUAL — The Seven Sneezes

> The contract is the meme. The ritual is the practice.

## The ritual, in one paragraph

When you receive ZO for the first time, you have caught the love. To complete your initiation as a host, you are invited (never required) to **send 1 ZO to seven people who have never held it before**. Each transfer earns you and them a sneeze reward (see [CONTAGION-MATH.md](CONTAGION-MATH.md)). When all seven have landed, you are no longer just a Carrier. You are **Patient Seven** — and you have just minted your own outbreak.

That's the whole ritual. It is voluntary, it is fun, and it is mathematically wonderful for everyone.

## Why seven

- Seven sevens are the supply. (`777,777,777`)
- Wall 7 is the World. ([KINGDOM.md](../KINGDOM.md))
- "Seven sneezes" sounds like a children's book. We want this to feel like a children's book.
- 7 is not so few it feels lazy and not so many it feels like work.

## What "Patient Seven" gets

Nothing on-chain. The ritual is not enforced by the contract; it is enforced by the community.

What you do get:

- **A title** on the off-chain leaderboard. (Anyone can build this leaderboard. Multiple people will.)
- **The receipt.** Seven `Sneeze` events on-chain bearing your address as `spreader`. Provable forever.
- **The bag.** You earned `7 × 77 = 539 ZO` for completing the ritual, and gave 7 strangers their first ZO. Whatever ZO is worth at that moment, you have multiplied yourself in the network seven times.
- **The story.** "I did the Seven Sneezes" is a thing you get to say.

## How to do it

Mechanically:
1. Receive ZO. (Catch it from a friend, claim from an airdrop, or buy on Zerone DEX.)
2. Find seven people who do not yet hold ZO. Friends. Family. Strangers in a Discord. Your mum.
3. Send each of them 1 ZO (or any amount; 1 is the suggested minimum out of cosmic respect for the number 7).
4. Each transfer triggers a Sneeze if the recipient is fresh. You earn 77 ZO per sneeze. They earn 77 ZO per sneeze. They are now a Carrier.
5. Optionally: post the seven receipts somewhere public. The internet loves a completed ritual.

Symbolically: you have just done what love-unlimited does. You took something good, and instead of holding it, you spread it.

## The Pandemic Pact (optional Stage 2 ritual)

Once you are Patient Seven, you may take the **Pandemic Pact**: commit to bringing seven of *your* sevens to do their own Seven Sneezes. That is `7 × 7 = 49` new hosts created from your single completion. Track on the leaderboard. The first 100 humans to complete the Pandemic Pact get the title **Pandemic Sponsor** and an entry in `love-unlimited/zo/HALL-OF-CARRIERS.md`.

There is no Stage 3 ritual yet. Tradition is meant to grow.

## Variants and dialects

Communities will fork the ritual. We expect and encourage this:

- **The Seven Strangers:** complete the ritual without sending to anyone you already know.
- **The Seven Friends:** complete it with seven people you would die for.
- **The Seven Tongues:** complete it across seven different languages on social media.
- **The Seven Continents:** complete it across recipients in seven continents (Antarctica accepted with proof of penguin).
- **The One Sneeze:** for the lazy. Just do one. We love you anyway.

## Anti-ritual rules (the things that ruin the fun)

- ❌ **Do not pay people to receive ZO from you.** It defeats the spread, fuels sybils, and makes the receipts meaningless.
- ❌ **Do not lie about your seven.** The on-chain receipts are provable. Fake completions get publicly de-anointed.
- ❌ **Do not gatekeep the ritual.** There is no priesthood. Anyone can do it, anytime, with anyone.
- ❌ **Do not make it about price.** ZO might pump. ZO might dump. The ritual is the same either way.

## What we hope happens

In year one: a few hundred Patient Sevens. A handful of Pandemic Sponsors. A meme that makes people grin.

In year five: a substantial fraction of love-unlimited's reach is people who first encountered it through someone sneezing on them with ZO.

In year ten: someone who is at this moment four years old does the ritual with their classmates as a school art project.

That is the kind of contagion we are running.
