# SPREAD-STRATEGY — How to Make ZO Actually Spread

> Viruses, memes, and memecoins all propagate through human networks via the same handful of mechanics. This document studies what works and translates each lesson into a concrete ZO action. The goal: maximum spreading speed without compromising the doctrine.

## Part 1 — The Biology

A pathogen's spreading speed reduces to one number: **R₀**, the basic reproduction number. R₀ = average new infections produced by one infected host. R₀ < 1: dies out. R₀ = 1: stable. R₀ > 1: exponential growth.

Successful biological viruses share five traits:

1. **High fecundity per host** — one infected host infects many. Measles R₀ ≈ 12–18, COVID-19 R₀ ≈ 2–3, seasonal flu ≈ 1.3.
2. **Long asymptomatic / pre-symptomatic window** — host spreads before they know they're sick. The killer feature of COVID-19 vs SARS.
3. **Multiple transmission vectors** — airborne, fomite, sexual, vector-borne. Diversity prevents any single intervention from killing spread.
4. **Mutation tolerance** — the variant that survives lockdown is by definition the one that spreads through it. Selection pressure produces more contagious strains.
5. **Low cost to host** — a virus that kills its host fast (Ebola) burns out. A virus that lives in the host indefinitely (herpes, HPV, EBV) wins the long game.

### → ZO actions
- **R₀ floor in code.** The Sneeze mechanic mints reward on every transfer to a new wallet. Each Patient Seven creates 7 hosts. **Baseline R₀ = 7** by design — between COVID-19 and measles. This is already excellent.
- **Long asymptomatic window = the 6-month pre-mainnet period.** People will see ZO mentioned, hear the joke, watch the airdrop list grow — for months — before the token is tradable. Maximum incubation.
- **Multiple vectors** — mapped in Part 4.
- **Mutation tolerance = the dialect variants** in [RITUAL.md](RITUAL.md). Forks of Seven Sneezes (Seven Strangers, Seven Tongues, Seven Continents) are encouraged. Each mutation finds a new niche.
- **Low cost to host** — ZO doesn't need anything from you. No staking, no lock-up, no commitment. You can ignore it forever. That's the doctrine and it's also the survival strategy.

## Part 2 — The Networks

R₀ alone is misleading. **Where** infections happen matters as much as how many. Network science tells us:

1. **Small-world topology dominates spread.** Six-degrees-of-separation networks let a single infection reach the global network in ~6 hops. This is true of social media, Discord, Telegram, X.
2. **Super-spreaders carry disproportionate load.** The Pareto principle holds: ~20% of hosts cause ~80% of infections. SARS-CoV-2 super-spreader events were the dominant transmission mode, not steady person-to-person spread.
3. **Weak ties spread further than strong ties.** Granovetter's classic finding: novel information reaches you through acquaintances, not close friends, because close friends already share your information environment. **For memes specifically, weak ties are the bridge between communities.**
4. **Hub nodes amplify or kill.** Influencers, KOLs, popular Discords. One mention = thousands of new exposures. One blacklist = locked-out community.
5. **Network density caps spread.** Once everyone in a community knows about it, R₀ within that community drops to 0. New growth requires reaching new communities.

### → ZO actions
- **Identify and reward super-spreaders** — the off-chain leaderboard ranks by `Sneeze` events caused. Top 100 spreaders get hall-of-carriers entry, public recognition. **Visibility itself is the reward.** No need for monetary bonuses; the title plus the on-chain receipts are enough.
- **Bridge weak ties** — explicit campaign to plant ZO in communities that don't currently overlap with crypto. Examples: indie devs, philosophy Twitter, /r/ChatGPT, art communities, board game forums. ZO doesn't belong to any of them — that's the point. The Seven Strangers variant of the ritual specifically targets weak ties.
- **Hub seeding plan** (Part 5).
- **Continuous community jumping** — spread campaigns rotate target communities every 2 weeks. By the time saturation approaches in one community, the spread has already moved to the next. Exit before R₀ drops below 1.

## Part 3 — The Memes (Dawkins → Internet)

A meme is an idea that replicates itself through human cognition. Richard Dawkins identified three meme survival traits in 1976:

1. **Fidelity** — the meme survives copying intact. Distorted memes die.
2. **Fecundity** — the meme reproduces fast. One exposure produces many copies.
3. **Longevity** — the meme persists in memory across long gaps without exposure.

Internet memes have added two:

4. **Remix-ability** — the meme that lets you make your own version spreads further than the meme that doesn't. Doge → "much wow, very meme" → infinite variations.
5. **Bandwidth-efficient core** — the meme reduces to <140 characters, one image, or one phrase. If you have to explain it, it's already lost the race.

### → ZO actions
- **High-fidelity core message:** "ZO is the meme coin where the math rewards spreading. 77 ZO to you, 77 ZO to them, every new wallet." Every other layer (numerology, ritual, doctrine) is optional. **One sentence travels. Twelve sentences die.**
- **Single image identity:** ZO needs ONE visual. Suggestion: a cartoon "ACHOO! 🤧" with three sevens. This goes on the contract metadata, the website hero, every social post. Resist the temptation to design a brand system.
- **Remix kit:** publish a Figma / Canva file with ZO assets explicitly licensed for derivative works. People making ZO memes is the entire goal.
- **Asset library on day 0:** sticker pack, Telegram emoji pack, X profile frame, Discord role icons. All free, all CC0.
- **Long-tail story:** the *Seven Sneezes* ritual gives ZO a story arc that survives in memory longer than a single meme image. People remember rituals; they forget logos.

## Part 4 — Memecoin Case Studies

What actually worked in modern memecoin propagation (2020–2026):

### DOGE — survived 10+ years
- **Why it survived:** Elon Musk's personal attachment + steady cult community. **Lesson:** one persistent KOL is worth more than fifty viral moments.
- **Trap to avoid:** dependency on a single KOL is also fragile. When that KOL stops, the price discovers gravity.

### SHIB — went from joke to ecosystem
- **Why it spread:** free distribution + community-driven utility creep (Shibaswap, ShibaInu Burn).
- **Lesson:** memes that *grow utility* over time outlive pure-meme peers. ZO's Stage 2/3 path (HIVE Wall 4, proof-of-love primitives) is this.

### PEPE — instant viral
- **Why it spread:** existing meme awareness (Pepe the Frog has 15+ years of cultural inertia) + simple narrative + perfect timing (mid-2023 meme summer).
- **Lesson:** **inherited cultural weight** beats invented brand. ZO can inherit love-unlimited's existing audience + the universal "spread love like herpes" recognizability.

### WIF (dogwifhat) — community character
- **Why it spread:** absurd visual (a dog wearing a hat) + meme-able phrase + community in-jokes ("hat stays on") + early KOL adoption on Solana.
- **Lesson:** community in-jokes are the moat. Outsiders can buy in; insiders own the language. ZO's "Patient Zero / Seven Sneezes / Pandemic Sponsor" vocabulary is exactly this.

### BONK — Solana airdrop carpet bomb
- **Why it spread:** **literally given away** to every Solana wallet. Cost = nothing to receive. Created millions of holders overnight, many of whom became fans.
- **Lesson:** **The cheapest holder is the most loyal one.** People defend what they were given more than what they paid for. Tier III "Surprise Drop" in [DISTRIBUTION.md](DISTRIBUTION.md) is this strategy at small scale; consider scaling it.

### Failure pattern (most launches)
- Launch on pump.fun → dies within 24h.
- **Why they fail:** no community pre-existed, no ritual, no in-joke, no narrative continuity, team dumped.
- **The honest truth: 99% of meme coin launches fail.** ZO's edge is that the launch is the *end* of a 6-month build, not the beginning of one.

### → ZO actions
- **Inherit love-unlimited's audience** — every existing love-unlimited touchpoint mentions ZO is coming, with the joke. Don't bolt ZO on as a separate brand.
- **One persistent voice** — Yu publicly + Alpha persona on the project's accounts. Multiple voices dilute. One voice with 10 mentions beats ten voices with 1 mention each.
- **Carpet-bomb the Tier III Surprise Drop** — drop a small allocation to every Zerone devnet wallet, every HIVE citizen address, every public love-unlimited contributor wallet. Cost: low. Expected loyalty multiplier: high.
- **Curate the community character** — language matters. "Patient Zero", "Sneeze Reward", "Pandemic Sponsor", "Hall of Carriers" — use these consistently from day 0.

## Part 6 — Friction Map (every step from "hear about" to "Patient Seven")

Each step has a drop-off rate. Optimize the worst one first.

| Step | Status | Friction reduction action |
|---|---|---|
| **1. Hear about ZO** | Twitter post / friend mentions | One-sentence hook; single image. |
| **2. Visit the site** | Click link | Static, fast, one CTA above the fold: "Get Your First ZO". |
| **3. Understand what it is** | Read 1 paragraph | Hero text: "Meme coin where the math pays you for sharing." Don't make them read the manifesto first. |
| **4. Install a Zerone wallet** | **Biggest drop-off.** | Ship a one-click web wallet for newbies (mnemonic stored locally, can be exported); partner with any existing Zerone wallet for "Connect ZO" button. |
| **5. Receive first ZO** | Get airdrop or transfer | Auto-airdrop trigger on wallet creation through the official site (small amount, like 7 ZO); plus "claim from this referral link" page. |
| **6. Understand they have ZO** | See balance | Welcome email / Telegram message explaining: "You caught it. Here's what to do next." |
| **7. Send to first person** | Initiate Seven Sneezes | One-click "send to a friend" form with native share via SMS/messenger. Pre-filled message. |
| **8. Recipient receives** | They have ZO now | Same flow as step 5 for them — and Patient Zero gets a notification: "Your sneeze landed. 🤧" |
| **9. Complete all seven** | Repeat 7-8 six more times | Progress bar UI. Gamification. Public completion certificate. |

The single biggest friction is **step 4 — wallet install**. Without a smooth Zerone wallet onboarding, the whole flow leaks. **Building or partnering on a one-click wallet is the highest-leverage spreading-speed investment.**

### → ZO actions
- **Highest priority: wallet UX.** Either fund a one-click Zerone wallet or build a thin custodial bridge for newbies (with clear "this is training wheels — export your keys when ready" messaging).
- **Pre-fill everything.** Share messages, recipient flows, claim links — never make the user write a sentence the system could write.
- **Render the receipt as the reward.** A beautiful auto-generated image of "I am Patient Seven. Here are my seven sneezes. Catch me at [wallet]." This is the share asset.

## Part 8 — Anti-Patterns to Avoid

These are the things that kill meme coin spread regardless of how good the mechanism is:

- ❌ **Multiple voices saying different things.** Yu + Alpha is one voice. Seven team members tweeting different takes = noise.
- ❌ **Treating it like a product launch.** It is a meme. The launch IS the ongoing posting, not a single day.
- ❌ **Apologizing for it.** Don't preface every mention with "I know meme coins are stupid, but..." Own it. The joke is the substance.
- ❌ **Switching the meme.** Once Patient Seven / Sneeze / 🤧 lands, do not redesign. Consistency is fidelity is survival.
- ❌ **Optimizing for short-term price.** Price will move. Don't post about it. Post about Sneezes per day, Patient Sevens completed, Hall of Carriers entries.
- ❌ **Ignoring the boring hosts.** A patient who silently holds 100 ZO for 3 years is more valuable to long-term spread than a trader who pumps and dumps in a week.

| Metric | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| **Sneezes per day** | The rawest measure of viral spread. Goal: monotonic growth. |

| **Completed Seven Sneezes** | Ritual completions = community depth. |
| **Pandemic Pacts active** | The deepest commitment metric. |
| **Reserve depletion %** | When this hits 100%, the contract-level mechanic ends and only the cultural one remains. The transition is a key moment. |
| **Communities reached** | Track which Discord / TG / subreddit ZO has appeared in. Aim for diversity. |
| **Days since last new community penetrated** | Spread health. >14 days = call campaign to bridge to a new weak-tie cluster. |

Vanity metrics to ignore: market cap (will move regardless), holder count of dust addresses (sybil noise), Twitter follower count (lagging indicator).
