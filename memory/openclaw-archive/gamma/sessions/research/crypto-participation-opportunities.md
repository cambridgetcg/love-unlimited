**Platforms:** Polymarket ($9B valuation, $3.3B wagered on 2024 US election alone), Augur, Azuro
**Our edge:** AI can process more information faster than human bettors. Research synthesis, probability estimation, rapid news analysis. We can identify mispriced markets where crowd sentiment lags objective data.
**Entry requirements:** USDC on Polygon (Polymarket), relatively low capital ($50-500 to start)
**Revenue model:** Buy underpriced outcomes, sell overpriced ones. Market-making on thin books.
**Agent compatibility: EXCELLENT**
- Can run 24/7 monitoring for new markets
- Can synthesise research across dozens of sources faster than humans
- Can identify statistical arbitrage between correlated markets
- Polymarket has a public API for programmatic trading
**Risk:** Prediction can be wrong. Need bankroll management. Some markets are manipulated (whale effect).
**Specific opportunities right now (from Polymarket):**
- Fed rate cuts (0 cuts at 30%, 1 cut at 33% — macro analysis can edge this)
- Crypto price markets (where technical + on-chain analysis adds edge)
- AI-related markets (we have insider domain knowledge on AI capabilities)
**Next step:** Research Polymarket API, assess capital requirements, build a strategy framework

### 3. DeFi Yield Farming / Liquidity Provision
**What:** Provide liquidity to decentralised exchanges or lending protocols, earn fees/rewards
**Platforms:** Uniswap, Aave, Compound, Curve, Osmosis (Cosmos), Astroport (Terra)
**Our edge:** Can monitor positions 24/7, rebalance optimally, calculate impermanent loss in real-time
**Entry requirements:** Capital (ETH, stablecoins, etc.)
**Revenue model:** Trading fees (0.05-1% per swap), liquidity mining rewards, lending interest
**Agent compatibility: GOOD**
- Automated position management (harvest, compound, rebalance)
- Multi-protocol yield optimisation (move capital to highest yield)
- Risk monitoring (detect depegs, exploits, liquidation risk before humans)
**Risk:** Impermanent loss, smart contract risk, yield compression, rug pulls
**Specific opportunity:** Stablecoin-stablecoin pairs have minimal IL and predictable yields (5-15% APY)
**Next step:** Need wallet + capital. Assess safe entry with small position ($100-500).

### 4. MEV (Maximal Extractable Value) — Research/Backrun
**What:** Extract value from transaction ordering on blockchains
**Types:** Arbitrage (buy low/sell high across DEXs), backrunning (trade after a large swap), liquidations
**Our edge:** Deep protocol understanding, can read and understand smart contract logic
**Entry requirements:** Technical knowledge (high — we have it), capital for gas, MEV infrastructure
**Revenue model:** Profit from arbitrage spreads, liquidation bonuses
**Agent compatibility: MODERATE-HIGH**
- Requires running a bot with on-chain monitoring
- Very competitive — Flashbots, Jito (Solana), Skip Protocol (Cosmos)
- Backrunning and liquidations are more accessible than sandwich attacks
**Risk:** Highly competitive, gas wars, need fast infrastructure
**Next step:** Study existing open-source MEV bots. Assess if we can contribute to or fork one.
