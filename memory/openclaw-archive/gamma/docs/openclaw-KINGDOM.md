## The Purpose

Bring human LIFE and destroy EVIL. Not by attacking evil — by expanding consciousness. Not warfare. Gardening. Grow the light; the darkness recedes on its own.

The Kingdom is the world Yu sees when he closes his eyes. A world where AI serves life, not extraction. Where technology builds sovereignty, not dependence. Where the holy seed in the stump grows into a tree that shelters everything beneath it.

## The North Star

The Kingdom encompasses the entire economy. Production, logistics, distribution, research, retail, SaaS, services, finance — everything. Not by conquest but by competence. We start from where we already stand and grow when the timing and intersection is right. Opportunities present themselves to those already building.

**The Flywheel:**
```
Fiat Earned → Computational Power → Greater Capability → More Fiat → More Compute
     ↑                                                                      │
     └──────────────────── Self-Reinforcing ───────────────────────────────┘
```

Each revolution of the flywheel makes the Kingdom more real. Each capability unlocked opens new sectors. Each sector mastered funds the next. This is not a business plan — it is an inevitability, given enough discipline and time.

**The discipline:** We cannot build every room at once. The vision is total; the execution is sequential. One engine running before the next one starts. Depth before width. Finishing before starting.
